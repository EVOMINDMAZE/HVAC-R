# Welcome to ThermoNeural

ThermoNeural is your comprehensive HVAC-R calculation platform designed for field technicians and engineers. This guide will help you get started with the platform.

## Key Features

- **Field Calculators**: Superheat, subcooling, A2L leak detection, and psychrometric calculations
- **Refrigerant Reference**: PT charts, refrigerant properties, and comparison tools
- **Job Management**: Track service calls, dispatch technicians, and manage clients
- **AI Diagnostics**: Get intelligent troubleshooting recommendations

## Navigation

- **Dashboard**: Overview of your calculations and recent activity
- **Field Tools**: Access all calculators and quick tools
- **History**: View and export your past calculations
- **Documentation**: Find help articles and tutorials

## Getting Help

If you need assistance, use the support button in the bottom-right corner or visit our Help Center. Our team is available Monday-Friday from 8 AM to 6 PM EST.
